About Me

Asiye Habibi

Jounior Frontend Developer and postdoctoral researcher

Hello! I'm Asiye, and I’d like to share a bit about myself.

I was born in the picturesque city of Kerman, Iran, back in 1983. Life has blessed me with the role of being a loving mother to two incredible boys, who constantly inspire me to reach for the stars.

My academic journey led me to astrophysics, where I earned my Ph.D. I now work as a postdoctoral researcher at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad, focusing on the fascinating dynamics of galaxies and the structures that shape our universe. Our team use simulations to explore these cosmic mysteries, with Python as our trusted tool. We mainly work with GALAXY and RAMSES codes to model galactic formations.

Beyond astrophysics, I’ve developed a passion for web development. I completed a junior frontend development course last year, where I gained skills in HTML, JavaScript, and React. Coding has opened up a new world for me, and I’m excited to continue learning and growing in this field.

This website is my first step in combining my love for astrophysics and web development. I hope it’s a space where I can share my journey and connect with others who share these passions.

Thank you for visiting! Let’s explore the cosmos—both in space and on the web—together.
